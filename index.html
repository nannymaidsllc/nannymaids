NannyMaids Client Agreement

By booking any service, you agree to all terms listed below.

Section 1

Payment Policy

Full payment is required before any service is scheduled. No exceptions. Your worker is assigned only after payment is confirmed. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Custom services are invoiced via Stripe after review.

Section 2

Cancellation Policy

24+ hours before appointment

Full refund or free reschedule.

Same-day cancellations

50% of total is non-refundable.

Less than 4 hours or no-show

No refund. Worker has been scheduled and dispatched.

Section 3

Service Satisfaction

If you are not satisfied with your service, you must report it within 24 hours of service completion. NannyMaids may offer a resolution at its discretion. Refunds are not guaranteed after a service has been completed.

Section 4

Non-Solicitation

Clients agree not to hire, contact, or engage in any direct service arrangement with any worker connected through NannyMaids for a period of 12 months from the last booking. Violation may result in removal from the platform.

Section 5

Independent Contractor Notice

All services are performed by independent contractors, not employees of NannyMaids LLC. NannyMaids coordinates and vets workers but does not directly employ them. Clients must provide a safe and accessible working environment.

Section 6

Liability

NannyMaids LLC is not liable for damages, losses, or incidents caused by independent contractors during service. NannyMaids serves as a coordination platform only.

Section 7

Agreement & Acceptance

By booking any service with NannyMaids and checking the agreement checkbox, you confirm you have read, understood, and agree to all terms listed in this agreement.
